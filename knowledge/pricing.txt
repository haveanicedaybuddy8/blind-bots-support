PRICING & SUBSCRIPTION MODEL

     * Fully customized AI Sales Bot trained on your specific company data.

     * SSL Security for data protection.
     * Automated Lead Forwarding (Transcripts sent to your email).
     * 100 Image Generation Credits included every month (Refills automatically on renewal).

2. ADD-ON: "Image Credits"
   - Price: $100 USD for a pack of 300 Credits.
   - Usage:

   - Note: You must ensure the email used at checkout matches your account email for credits to apply instantly.

3. BILLING LOGIC
